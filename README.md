# Hi, I'm Mark Lannaman 🌎🛰️
**Geospatial Insights  | Storyteller | Clean Energy Advocate**

### Enviornment & Energy
I turn satellite (remote sensing) and georeferenced data (GIS) into interactive tools and compelling visual stories to advance energy equity, sustainable development, and ecological conservation. I'm currently an ORISE fellow for the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE), helping to advance community-led energy transitions and equity. I'm also NASA DEVELOP alumni, where a team and I leveraged Earth observations for regional partner decision-making. Before these, I was a graduate student at Georgia Tech where I earned an M.S. in Environmental Engineering, M.S. in Sustainable Energy & Environmental Management, and M.S. in Global Media & Cultures. 
My Tech Stack:
* **Geospatial Tools:** Google Earth Engine (GEE), QGIS, DuckDB
* **Programming Langues:** Python (Panel, pandas, numpy, geemap, leafmap); JavaScript (GEE).
* **Research Interests:** Renewable Energy Microgrids; Environmental Monitoring & Conservaiton; Closed-Environment Agriculture Technology (CEA Systems); Urban Land Use Change
